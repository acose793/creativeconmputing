Hannes von Döhren / 2010

Released in 2010, Brandon Grotesque is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by German type designer Hannes von Döhren of HVD Fonts. The design was heavily influenced by the geometric typefaces that originated in the 1920s, taking a little more influence from Erbar rather than the
more well-known design from that time period, Futura.

* Influenced by the geometric-style sans serif faces that were popular during the 1920s and 30s *
